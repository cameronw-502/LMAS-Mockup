Louisville Metro Animal Services

Louisville Metro Animal Services is committed to saving more shelters lives by making every effort to find homes for our adoptable pets. When you adopt from LMAS, you will be rescuing a homeless pet from your community. LMAS has found homes for thousands of pets through free and donation-based adoption programs.

Donate to L.M.A.S

Friends of Metro Animal Services (FOMAS) seeks aid from our gracious community to help us provide a home to our abandoned and injured dogs, cats, horses, and pigs of Louisville Metro Animal Services (LMAS). We are the only open-admission shelter in Jefferson County, Kentucky, and we do not turn away any animal due to overcrowded space; our doors are open to all. FOMAS is a local non-profit agency and donations made to us help the homeless pets in our own community.
